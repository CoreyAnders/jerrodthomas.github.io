For my final project, I chose to research how the internet has affected genealogical research. I will include how genealogy was done prior to the internet, once the internet became available, and what the future could hold for genealogical research. Before we begin, I want to highlight a few things about genealogy. I'm not going to spend time on these, it's not the purpose of my research.

What is genealogy?

Genealogy is simply the study and tracing a person's ancestors.

Why do people do genealogy?


People do genalogy for many reaons including
  • To remember and honor their ancestors
  • Curiosity about their ancestors
  • To learn of any family history of medical conditions
  • to find their birth parents
  • Religious tenents such as The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints
